Architecture

Three layers, one source of truth

Each layer serves its own purpose — but data flows through all of them automatically.

01
The Open Floor
Shared team channels where everything happens. A rep writes one sentence — the deal updates, the team learns, the history is saved.
02
AI Agents
Six specialised agents translate natural language into structured data. Deal Updater, Case Manager, Offer Drafter, Forecast Narrator, and more.
03
Role-based dashboards
The sales manager sees stalled deals at a glance. Finance sees the time-phased forecast. All auto-populated as the team works.
Deal UpdaterUpdates stages from conversation
Case ManagerCreates cases and starts the SLA timer
Offer DrafterBuilds offers from the product catalog
Forecast NarratorExplains pipeline health in plain language
Next-Best-ActionSuggests the next step per account
Meeting ScribeExtracts action items from meeting notes
